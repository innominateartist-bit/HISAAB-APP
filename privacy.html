🔒 Hisaab — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Hisaab ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, phone number, email address, city, and password (stored as encrypted hash, never in plain text)
  • Business Data: Inventory items, transaction records (khata), shipment details that you voluntarily enter
  • Chat Messages: Messages exchanged within the app (phone numbers and emails are automatically blocked/redacted)
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for app optimization

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account with a unique User ID
  • To provide trade ledger, inventory, and shipment tracking features
  • To enable secure masked communication between traders
  • To generate Bharosa (trust) scores based on transaction history
  • To send OTP for account recovery when you forget your password

3. Data Protection & Security

  • Passwords are hashed — never stored in plain text
  • Phone numbers and emails are automatically masked in all communications
  • All inputs are sanitized against XSS (cross-site scripting) attacks
  • Each user's data is stored separately using their unique User ID
  • Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Contact information sharing is blocked in chat messages

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your business data (inventory, transactions, shipments) is visible only to you when logged into your account.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below. Upon deletion request, all your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Hisaab is designed for business use by adults (18 years and older). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

7. OTP & Account Recovery

When you use the "Forgot Password" feature, a one-time password (OTP) is sent to your registered phone number or email address. This OTP is valid for a single use and expires after verification.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
